        5 Surprising facts about Tollywoods' Prince Mahesh-Babu!

        Hemachandra Ethamukkala | Last updated on - Aug 9, 2021, 12:38 ISTShare fbsharetwsharepinshareComments (0)

          close

        01/65 Surprising facts about Mahesh Babu

        Shop Similar Look

        Mahesh Babu Ghattamaneni is a well Telugu actor, producer, and philanthropist from Telugu cinema. He mainly works in Tollywood and is known for his charismatic looks, family subjects, and stardom. Many of his movies have been remade in other film industries due to their good content and success.

        Fans dearly call him ‘Prince’ in Tollywood who never chooses to do remake films. He made his Telugu film debut with ‘Rajakumarudu’ in 1999 under the direction of K.Raghavendra Rao, till now Mahesh has starred in over 25 movies in the lead role, over these years he has achieved several unique milestones, let us check a few of them below.

        readmore

        02/6First Telugu Actor to get a Wax Statue at Madame Tussauds.

        Shop Similar Look

        Mahesh Babu is the first Telugu cum South Indian actor to get a 'Wax Statue' At 'Madame Tussauds' In 2019. His wax statue was unveiled in Madame Tussauds Singapore in the year 2019. He was also the first Indian actor whose wax statue was flown back for a day for unveiling at his hometown.

        while Baahubali (portrayed by Prabhas) was the first Telugu film character to get a Wax Statue at Madame Tussauds.

        readmore

        03/6​Introduced ‘Dolby EX Surround Sound System’ in Telugu Cinema.

        Shop Similar Look

        Like his father, who is often credited for producing many technological firsts in the Telugu film industry such as

        1.The first Eastmancolor film – Eenadu (1982),

        2.The first cinemascope film – Alluri Seetharama Raju (1974),

        3.The first 70mm film – Simhasanam (1986),

        4.The first DTS film – Telugu Veera Levara (1995) and

        5.The first to introduce the cowboy genre to the Telugu screen.

        Mahesh Babu introduced the new ‘Dolby EX Surround Sound System technology‘ to the Telugu cinema, through his film “Nijam” under the direction of Teja.

        readmore

        04/6​Forever Desirable

        Shop Similar Look

        He was ranked 12th on the ‘Times' 50 Most Desirable Men in India for the year 2010’, fifth in 2011, and second in 2012. He climbed to the first position on the above list for the year 2013, beating the likes of Bollywood superstars such as 'Hrithik Roshan, Shah Rukh Khan & Salman Khan'. He stood at sixth place for the following year 2014, remained in the same position in 2015, seventh in 2016, climbed up to sixth in 2017.

        He was then added to the “Forever Desirable list” along with Hrithik Roshan, Akshay Kumar, Shah Rukh Khan, Aamir Khan, and Salman Khan.

        readmore

        05/6Most followed Southern Star on Twitter

        Shop Similar Look

        He joined Twitter in April 2010 and has over 11.5 million followers as of today. Mahesh has reached this mark in 2021. Mahesh is the first & only south Indian superstar to have achieved this feat so far, followed by Dhanush with more than 10 million followers.

        readmore

        06/6​Only Superstar of Telugu cinema with ‘No Remakes’

        Shop Similar Look


        Even after completing 20 years in his film career. Mahesh Babu has never opted for a single remake from other languages. He made his acting debut as a lead in the K Raghavendra Rao directed-film Rajakumarudu in 1999. The superstar also once publicly announced that he would never do any remakes and spoil their greatness.

        The actor who is waiting for his next release with Parasuram directorial “Sarkaru Vaari Paata”, is a commercial action entertainer which is an original film that stars Mahanati fame, Keerthy Suresh, playing his love interest to him in the film slated for 2022 Sankranthi Release.
